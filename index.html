DESIRES Lab @ Peking University

DEciSion, computIng, eneRgy, and Economic Systems (DESIRES) Lab is directed by Prof. Pengcheng You at the Department of Industrial Engineering and Management, College of Engineering, Peking University. We work in the intersection of engineering and economics, with a focus on analysis and design for real-world systems with provable performance. Particular research interests include

  • Optimization from a Dynamical System Perspective: Minimax Optimization, Saddle Flow Dynamics

  • Reinforcement Learning: Constrained/Safe RL, Hybrid Transfer RL, Multi-Objective RL

  • Learning for Control: Structured Neural Networks, Deep Koopman Learning

  • Market Mechanism Design: Multi-Timescale Markets, Market Dynamics, Market Power Mitigation, Emerging Distributed Energy Resource Technologies

  • Power and Energy Systems: Frequency/Voltage Regulation, Electricity Markets, Electric Vehicles, Energy Storage
